The Problem
1 in 3
pedestrian accidents involve
distracted walking with headphones
🎧

Headphones block critical audio cues

You can't hear oncoming trains, car horns, or warning signals when music is playing at full volume.

📞

Calls & meetings are just as dangerous

Walking through a railway crossing mid-Zoom call or phone conversation is as risky as blasting music — your attention is split and volume blocks ambient sound.

📱

Manual pausing is unreliable

Reaching for your phone at a crossing takes attention away from the road at the worst possible moment.

🗺️

Riga has 47+ high-risk crossings

Railway crossings and busy intersections throughout the city — most pedestrians don't know them all.

How It Works

Three steps.
Zero effort.

01
📍

We track your location

CrossAware uses GPS to know exactly where you are. All data stays on your phone — nothing is sent to any server.

02
🏃

We detect your speed

Walking at 4 km/h? Cycling at 18 km/h? CrossAware classifies your movement and only activates when you're on foot or on a bike.

03
⏸️

Music pauses automatically

Within 15 metres of a danger zone, your music pauses instantly. Once you're clear, it resumes automatically.
