The Plugin

Invisible to riders.
Invaluable to you.

Pause & Pass runs entirely in the background of your app. No UI. No banners. No "powered by" badge. Riders experience seamless audio management as a natural part of your product — they never know the plugin is there.

🛵
Trip starts

Rider opens your app

They scan the scooter, start the trip — exactly as normal. Plugin activates silently in the background. The rider sees nothing different.

🎵
Crossing zone

Plugin fires silently

Geofencing engine detects the zone. OS-level audio paused instantly. No popup. No alert. No indication whatsoever. Just silence — exactly when it matters.

✅
Crossing cleared

Audio resumes. Event logged.

Music resumes exactly where it left off. Intervention logged to your dashboard. Rider never encountered a single pixel of Pause & Pass.

Step 01

Add the plugin

Your engineer adds the Pause & Pass SDK to your existing Android or iOS project. One import, one initialisation call with your operator key. Nothing else changes in your app.

npm install @pausepass/sdk
Step 02

Hook into trip events

Attach to your existing trip start and end callbacks. The plugin runs entirely in the background — geofencing, GPS monitoring, zone detection. Your app UI is completely untouched.

PausePass.startSession(trip)
Step 03

Done. Fleet protected.

Every ride in your fleet is now covered. Safety events flow silently into your operator dashboard. Riders benefit without knowing it. You own the record. We stay invisible.
